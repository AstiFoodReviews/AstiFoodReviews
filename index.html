About this website

This website is a project by ASTI students created to recommend students restaurants to eat at during lunch. This website is still a work-in-progress, so it would be greatly appreciated if you could fill out this short form to give feedback or report bugs. Thanks for checking out this website!

Habit Burger & Grill

4.00

A burger chain that originated from Santa Barbara, CA. Habit serves charburgers, their signature Santa Barbara sandwich, fries, onion rings, and more.

RareTea

3.75

RareTea is a boba store located in Alameda landing that serves a variety of drinks and snacks.

Wescafe

3.00

Wescafe, located on Webster St, is a small cozy coffeehouse featuring baked goods, sandwiches & java drinks plus ample courtyard seating.

Mango & Mint

3.00

Located on Webster St, Mango & Mint is a very small casual restaurant serving Vietnamese staples such as banh mi and vermicelli bowls, plus vegetarian options.

In-N-Out

4.00

This classic burger chain, located near Alameda Landing, serves burgers, hand-cut fries & shakes.

Ono Hawaiian BBQ

3.25

A nice restaurant in Alameda Landing that serves plate lunches served with rice & macaroni salad as the mainstays of this Hawaiian-inspired chain.
