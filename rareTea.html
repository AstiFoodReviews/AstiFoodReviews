Rare Tea

Overall Rating

3.75 / 5

Boba

$5-10

Walkable

Short Wait

Raretea sometimes has long wait times, but there are two places to order not including online. They have a short wait time for drinks, around 2-4 minutes per drink. Food takes a little longer being 2 minutes to 5 minutes depending on what you order. The drinks are pretty good and the food is fresh as it’s made to order. Additionally, there’s a lot of claw machines you can play on while you wait.
